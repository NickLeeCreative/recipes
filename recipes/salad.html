Description

Not gonna lie, salads are pretty easy. If I was a better man, I'd have a specific recipe ready off the top of my head. But I'm not, and I don't.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Whatever else you want

Steps

  1. Rinse all your produce-based ingredients. No one wants listeria.
  2. Chop up the lettuce and anything else that isn't already bite-size.
  3. If you have beans, make sure they're ready (including rinsing canned beans).
  4. Combine everything, and top with dressing of your choice.
